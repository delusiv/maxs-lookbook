Scene 1: Falling

    • ext. SKY – DAY
      JON in a bright-green-cyan protective suit drifts downward, arms limp, clearly asleep as he freefalls. The roar of the wind is deafening, but somehow, his high-tech watch buzzes through. The WEEDMAPS AI ASSITANT begins to speak.
      WEEDMAPS AI ASSISTANT
      (V.O., cheerful)
      Hey Jon! Rise and shine! We’ve got potential customers on the radar!
      Jon’s eyelids flutter in the violent wind.
      JON
      (mumbling)
      Five more minutes...
      WEEDMAPS AI ASSISTANT
      John, I’m detecting a high probability event: two nearby customers need a better cannabis deal. Let’s make that money!
      A holographic display on the watch flickers to life, zooming in to reveal DANTE and JACK, sitting in a parked car, eating McDonald’s. The camera pushes in until we enter the watch’s view.
