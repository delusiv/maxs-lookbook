Scene 2: My Pleasure

    • INT./EXT. DANTE’S CAR – DAY
      Dante leans back in the passenger seat.
      DANTE
      You know, Every time I buy a vape at Circle K, the clerk says “My pleasure,” like I'm at Chick-fil-A.
      Jack, busy unwrapping a burger, looks over, confused.
      JACK
      'My pleasure'?
      DANTE
      (animated)
      Yeah, I'll go to the same store with the same employee buy chips. Nothing. Go again to buy a vape.'My pleasure'.
      Suddenly, Dante’s phone buzzes. He glances down and his eyes go wide.
      DANTE
      (excited whisper)
      Holy shit.
      DANTE
      Remember that $50 parlay I placed this morning?
      JACK
      (bored)
      Yeah?
      DANTE
      (grinning)
      It just turned it into $450.
      JACK
      ...
      DANTE
      You'll be able to talk when you hit a parlay.
      Dante crumples the wrapper and throws it across the dash, feeling proud.
